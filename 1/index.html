Coarsest Image
- Downsample original image such that its shortest side is rescaled to be as close to 100 as possible
- Try displacements within +/- 8% of image width and length (naive implementation)

Coarse-to-Fine Image Pyramid
- Scale up coarse images (and optimal displacements) by a factor of 2
- Try displacements within +/- 1% of optimal displacement
- Significantly reduces alignment speed

Image Matching Evaluation Metric
- NCC: dot product between two normalized vectors
- SSIM: structural similarity index (considers luminance, contrast structure)
- Performs better for images like emir.tif, but runs 4x slower
